Contract Metadata

Project Name:Metropolitan Shopping Center Development
Client Name:Urban Development Corporation
Contractor:Premier Commercial Builders Ltd
Contract Value:$28,950,000 USD
Start Date:June 15, 2024
End Date:May 30, 2026
Country:United Arab Emirates
Payment Terms:Bi-weekly progress payments within 21 days of invoice
1. PROJECT SCOPE

The Contractor shall design, construct, and deliver a modern commercial retail complex comprising 150,000 square meters of retail space, entertainment facilities, dining areas, and supporting infrastructure including parking for 2,500 vehicles.

List of Services:

  • Architectural design and space planning
  • Structural engineering and foundation work
  • Main retail building construction (120,000 m²)
  • Entertainment complex construction (30,000 m²)
  • Multi-level parking structure (50,000 m²)
  • HVAC system design and installation
  • Electrical and lighting systems
  • Fire safety and security systems
  • Retail fit-out coordination
  • Landscaping and exterior amenities
  • 12-month defects liability period

3. CONTRACTUAL OBLIGATIONS

3.1 Bi-weekly Progress Reports

Description: Submit detailed bi-weekly progress reports including construction status, quality metrics, safety records, and financial tracking with photographic evidence.

Frequency: Bi-weekly

Due Date: Every other Friday by 3:00 PM

Penalty: $7,500 for each day late

3.2 Weekly Safety Inspections

Description: Conduct comprehensive weekly safety inspections of all work areas, equipment, and personnel compliance with safety protocols.

Frequency: Weekly

Due Date: Every Monday by 10:00 AM

Penalty: $15,000 per missed inspection + work suspension

3.3 Monthly Quality Audits

Description: Perform monthly quality audits covering workmanship, materials compliance, and adherence to specifications with third-party verification.

Frequency: Monthly

Due Date: Last working day of each month

Penalty: 3% payment reduction for failed audits + remedial work costs

3.4 Environmental Compliance Monitoring

Description: Monitor and maintain environmental compliance including noise control, dust management, and waste disposal according to local regulations.

Frequency: Daily

Due Date: Daily by 6:00 PM

Penalty: $12,000 per violation + regulatory fines

3.5 Retail Space Delivery

Description: Complete retail spaces according to tenant requirements and approved specifications, ensuring all utilities and systems are operational.

Frequency: Per delivery schedule

Due Date: As per tenant occupancy timeline

Penalty: $25,000 per day delay per retail unit

3.6 System Testing and Commissioning

Description: Test and commission all building systems including HVAC, electrical, fire safety, and security systems with full documentation.

Frequency: Per system completion

Due Date: 30 days before tenant move-in

Penalty: $40,000 per day delay in operational readiness

3.7 Parking Facility Management

Description: Ensure parking facility construction meets capacity requirements and traffic flow specifications with proper signage and safety features.

Frequency: Milestone-based

Due Date: 90 days before mall opening

Penalty: $20,000 per parking space below requirement

3.8 Water System Maintenance

Description: Maintain water systems including plumbing, sprinkler systems, and decorative fountains with minimal spillage and optimal pressure.

Frequency: Daily

Due Date: Continuous monitoring

Penalty: $3,000 per day for spillage exceeding 3 liters

3.9 Tenant Coordination Services

Description: Coordinate with individual tenants for fit-out requirements, utility connections, and space modifications according to lease agreements.

Frequency: As required per tenant schedule

Due Date: Per individual tenant agreements

Penalty: $10,000 per tenant delay + tenant compensation costs

3.10 Emergency Response Planning

Description: Develop and maintain comprehensive emergency response plans and conduct monthly emergency drills with all personnel.

Frequency: Monthly

Due Date: 15th of each month

Penalty: $18,000 per missed drill + safety compliance review

4. PENALTY CLAUSES

4.1 Liquidated Damages

For delays beyond the contract completion date: 0.3% of total contract value per week, capped at 8% of total contract value.

4.2 Quality Non-Compliance

Failure to meet quality standards will result in mandatory remedial work at contractor's expense plus 5% penalty on affected work value.

4.3 Safety Incidents

Major safety incidents: $75,000 + work suspension. Minor incidents: $8,000 per occurrence.

4.4 Tenant Delay Penalties

Delays affecting tenant move-in schedules will result in contractor liability for lost rent and tenant compensation.

5. PAYMENT TERMS

Payments shall be made bi-weekly based on certified progress, subject to 5% retention until practical completion and additional 5% until end of defects liability period.

Payment Milestones:

  • Contract Execution: 5% mobilization payment
  • Foundation Completion: 15% of contract value
  • Structural Framework: 25% of contract value
  • Building Envelope: 20% of contract value
  • MEP Installation: 20% of contract value
  • Finishes and Fit-out: 10% of contract value
  • Final Handover: 5% final payment
